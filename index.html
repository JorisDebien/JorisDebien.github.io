My career

After a change in IT management I applied for the opportunity to become Head of Delivery. I lead 5 feature teams (around 30 people), identifying & experimentally tackling sources of blocking issues, lack of communication between silos, lack of knowledge sharing, etc. I also slowly started to lead the teams from a cost-driven to an outcome-driven mindset.

Together with the rest of IT Management I identified key blocking factors in our organisation and executed on a strategy to remove or reduce these factors.
